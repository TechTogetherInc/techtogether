Chapter Coordinator

TechTogether works to elevate the access, exposure, and inclusion of gender-marginalized communities in the interdisciplinary fields of technology. By hosting events like hackathons, promoting inclusivity-focused research, and providing career development resources, we strive to make dream careers more accessible to gender-marginalized students. We currently have a community of over 3,000+ students and two chapters in Boston and New York.

As the Chapter Coordinator, you will be the primary contributor to growing TechTogether’s dynamic and exciting growth. You will be responsible for setting existing and future chapters up for long-term success. This is a volunteer position fit for individuals passionate about achieving gender parity across the tech sector.

Responsibilities

  • Expand, recruit, and open new chapters while building relationships with current chapter leaders
  • Educate chapter leads on TechTogether’s mission, function, and programs
  • Create systems and procedures for chapter recruitment, engagement, and management.
  • Work with the TechTogether team to create chapter support documents and materials
  • Work with the TechTogether’s marketing team to engage chapter members

Recommended Experience

  • Experience participating in or organizing hackathons
  • Educate chapter leads on TechTogether’s mission, function, and programs
  • You are independent and self-sufficient
  • Passionate about TechTogether’s mission
  • Effective communicator both orally and written

Benefits

  • Grow your network of peers, mentors, and advocates
  • Develop your leadership and project management skills
  • Join our supportive team for a sense of community
  • Travel for free to annual chapter events across the country
