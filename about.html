TechTogether offers free pathways for young adults to discover interdisciplinary areas of technology. We aim to inspire our community and provide them with the confidence to pursue their curiosities.

TechTogether works to elevate the access, exposure, and inclusion of gender-marginalized students in the interdisciplinary fields of technology. By hosting events like hackathons, promoting inclusivity-focused research, and providing professional development resources, we strive to make dream careers more accessible to gender-marginalized communities.

TechTogether was originally founded in 2017 as SheHacks by a group of 13 students across Boston. What was once a one-time event has transformed into a nonprofit after the founders realized the impact gender-focused events had on gender-marginalized communities.
