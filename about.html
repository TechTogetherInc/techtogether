Innovation is
optimized by
inclusion.

TechTogether works to elevate the access, exposure, and inclusion of gender-marginalized individuals in the interdiciplinary fields of technology.

By hosting events like hackathons, promoting inclusivity-focused research, and providing career development resources, we strive to make a dream of a career in technology more accessible to gender-marginalized communities.

According to a 2019 research study, "41 percent of women report having an experience in tech that makes then less likely to attend an event in the future."

Inclusivity in tech is not about providing equal opportunity, but also diversity in perspective. The interdiciplinary feilds of technology are among the most influential forces of designing the future of humankind.

If marginalized people do not have a seat at this table...

For this reason, TechTogether (previously known as SheHacks) was founded in 2018 with the intention of ending the gender gap in technology.
