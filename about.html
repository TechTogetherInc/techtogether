TechTogether works to elevate the access, exposure, and inclusion of gender-marginalized individuals in the interdiciplinary fields of technology.

By hosting events like hackathons, promoting inclusivity-focused research, and providing career development resources, we strive to make a dream of a career in technology more accessible to gender-marginalized communities.
