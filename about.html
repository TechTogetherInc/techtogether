According to a 2019 research study,
"41 percent of women report having an experience in tech that makes then less likely to attend an event in the future."

Inclusivity in tech is not only about providing equal opportunity, but also diversity in perspective. The interdisciplinary fields of technology are among the most influential forces designing the future of humankind. If marginalized people do not have a seat at this table, the dangerous impacts will reverberate in every area of society from public health imbalance to environmental catastrophe.

TechTogether works to elevate the access, exposure, and inclusion of gender-marginalized students in the interdisciplinary fields of technology. By hosting events like hackathons, promoting inclusivity-focused research, and providing professional development resources, we strive to make dream careers more accessible to gender-marginalized communities.
