TechTogether's First Annual Hacker Awards

Virtual • March 13th, 2021 • 7- 10pm EST

TechTogether's first awards ceremony. A virtual celebratation of your success this 2020-2021 hackathon season.

Celebrating Excellence in the TechTogether Community

The courage of our first-time hackers, generosity of our sponsors, kindness of our volunteers, and the altrusim of our organizers. You all are the force that drives TechTogether's mission forward. The Annual Hacker Awards are a celebration of all that we have accomplished as a collective this year.
Life is full of firsts. This year's event theme, The First But Not the Last, celebrates the milestones we have made together towards gender equality.
Whether you were nominated for an award or attending to support a friend, we hope you will join us for this momentus event in TechTogether's history. Each ticket costs one $10 minimum donation to TechTogether and includes access to our incredible keynotes, trivia networking session, and musical performance. All ticket revenue will be doubled by a donor and go to expanding TechTogether's impact. Scholarships are available upon request.
Award recipients receive a certificate of achievement and an invitation to a private virtual dinner with all food expenses covered by TechTogether. Nominations must be made by X date, registrations will close on X date.

Awards

Fire-Starter Award

The Firestarter Award will be awarded to a high school hacker that has distinguished themselves through their courage, professional growth, and contributions to the TechTogether community.

Organizer Award

The Oustanding Organizer Award will be awarded to the organizer who has distinguished themselves through their contributions to the TechTogether community and legacy.

Alumni Award

The Alumni Award will be awarded to a TechTogether alumn who has distinguished themselves through their professional accomplishments.

Mentor Award

The Mentor Award is designed to recognize a volunteer that has provided outstanding service to the TechTogether community.

Hacker Award

The Hacker Award will be awarded to the team with the most outstanding TechTogether hackathon project.

Advocate Award

The Advocate Award is designed to recognize a company that has provided outstanding service to the TechTogether community.

Resilience Award

The Advocate Award will be awarded to a hacker from an untraditional background that has distinguished themselves through their courage, professional growth, and contributions to the TechTogether community.

Frequently Asked Questions

If you were nominated for an award, our team will let you know by Monday, February 15th.
We will announce all the winners at the event. If you are unable to attend, we will send you an email or LinkedIn note.
Award winners will not have to give a speech.
Anyone can attend this event. Unlike our hackathons, we have no gender or age restrictions on this event.
This laptop will take place over zoom. All you need to participate is a laptop and access to wifi. If you don't have one or both of these resources, please email us at info@techtogether.io.
Scholarships will cover the ticket cost of the event. The application deadline for the scholarship program is February 15th. Scholarships will be first come first serve. We will notify you by February 22nd if you were awarded one of the scholarships.
Read the award description for qualification details. For any questions, please email us at info@techtogether.io.
You do not need to attend the entire event, however the event will not be recorded.
