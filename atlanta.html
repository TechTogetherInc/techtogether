TechTogether Atlanta

TechTogether Atlanta was TechTogether's fourth chapter location, opened in 2020 along with TechTogether Seattle.

TechTogether Atlanta is the only gender-focused hackathon located in Georgia State.

TechTogether Atlanta serves primarily high school and college students in Georgia with a focus on Atlanta. The organizing team consists of leaders from schools in Georgia.
