TechTogether Atlanta

TechTogether Atlanta was TechTogether's fourth chapter location, opened in 2020 along with TechTogether Seattle.

TechTogether Atlanta is the only gender-focused hackathon located in Georgia.

TechTogether Atlanta serves primarily high school, college, and bootcamp students in the Georgia area with a focus on Atlanta. The organizing team consists of leaders from around Georgia.
