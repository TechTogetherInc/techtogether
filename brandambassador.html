TechTogether Brand Ambassador

Deadline to Apply: September 27th, 2020
Position Duration: October 4th, 2020 - February 15th, 2021

TechTogether is a nonprofit whose mission is to elevate the access, exposure, and inclusion of gender-marginalized students in interdisciplinary fields of technology. By hosting events like hackathons, promoting inclusivity-focused research, and providing professional development resources, we strive to make dream careers more accessible to gender-marginalized communities.

TechTogether is looking for Brand Ambassadors to help expand the TechTogether community and grow awareness of TechTogether events! This program is a 6 month position.
