TechTogether Seattle

TechTogether Seattle was TechTogether's third chapter location, opened in 2020 along with TechTogether Atlanta.

TechTogether Seattle is the only gender-focused hackathon located in Washington State.

TechTogether Seattle serves primarily high school and college students in the Northeast area with a focus on Seattle. The organizing team consists of leaders from Washington.
