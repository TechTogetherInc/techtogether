About the Program

Since 2017, TechTogether has served over 3,500 people of marginalized genders through our annual Chapter program. This program is a good fit for candidates looking to give back to their community, grow their professional network, build their resume, and sharpen their professional skill-sets.

The Chapter program trains all members on how to host a TechTogether gender-focused hackathon. TechTogether has been hosting hackathons for 4 years with an average 99% recommendation rating.

Organizer Perks

All organizers will get access to the following benefits:

  • Alumni Referral Portal
  • Internal TechTogether Events
  • Alumni Slack Channel
  • Alumni Newsletter
  • TechTogether Swag Pack

This year, TechTogether is implementing a new training and onboarding program. This program will be held over the course of 3 weeks and help you gain the skills needed to be a TechTogether organizer.

The TechTogether alumni referral portal includes referrals to companies like Audible, Microsoft, Amazon, Google, Drift, The Boston Globe, Capital One, Discover, and more.
