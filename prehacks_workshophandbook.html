Frequently Asked Questions

It's quite simple, you are responsible for leading the workshop that you signed up for on the PreHacks website at the time we assigned you.
You do not need to purchase any resources to be a workshop lead at our event. We will provide you with all the resources you need to be successful in this role. Attendees will be notified prior to the workshop if they need to prepare or download anything.
We will send out a zoom link and workshop details to the attendees 10-minutes prior to each workshop. We ask that workshop leads arrive 5 minutes prior to their assigned workshop time. A TechTogether volunteer will be at every workshop to help you setup and moderate the workshop as needed. You will be given the option to record to your workshop, if you'd like the information to remain a resource for event attendees.
You should arrive 5 minutes before your workshop begins to get setup and acquainted with your TechTogether volunteer.
You do not need to register for the event, but we would love to have you share the registration link with your network.
We ask that each workshop be no more than 45 minutes long, unless you've specified otherwise with the organizing team. If your workshop is going to be longer than 45 minutes and you haven't already let me know, please let me know as soon as possible.
