Hosted by Partnership with Boston University and BU Spark!

PreHacks is designed to give high schoolers an introduction to hackathons, coding, and a career in STEM.

PreHacks is a free two-day program co-hosted with Boston University and BU Spark! for women (cis and trans) and non-binary high schoolers to learn more about career paths in STEM and learn coding fundamentals. The goal of this program is to get you the resources and information you need to thrive at your next hackathon.
At PreHacks Seattle, 100% said they would recommend PreHacks to a friend and 84% said PreHacks increased their confidence in attending future hackathons.
This program is designed for those in the Eastern Time Zone (EST). If you need help converting the time difference, please use this resource.

You can attend as much of as little of the event as you'd like. We understand that not everyone can dedicate a full-weekend to the program.

PreHacks is not a hackathon, but a program to help prepare you to attend future hackathons.
