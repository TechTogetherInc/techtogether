An Introduction to Hackathons, Coding, and a Career in Technology.

We get it. Hackathons, coding, technology - all these things are scary as a young beginner on the outside, looking in. At PreHacks, our aim is to help high schoolers conquer these fears and ace their next hackathon. This program is designed to serve people of marginalized genders.
PreHacks is a FREE one day virtual learnathon consisting of non-stop workshops and activities led by the TechTogether team. By the end of the program, each attendee will walk away with a basic understanding of hackathons and programming fundamentals.
Last year, 99% of attendees said they would recommend PreHacks to a friend and 81% said PreHacks increased their confidence in attending future hackathons.

What is the difference between a learnathon and a hackathon?


As its name suggests, a learnathon is focused on learning and is designed to help introduce participants to the world of tech and the hacker community.
