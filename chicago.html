TechTogether Chicago

TechTogether Chicago was TechTogether's sixth chapter location, opened in 2021 along with TechTogether Miami.

TechTogether Chicago is one of the few gender-focused hackathons located in Illinois.

TechTogether Chicago serves primarily high school, college, and bootcamp students in Illinois and Missouri with a focus on Chicago. The organizing team consists of students and residents from across both Illinois and Missouri.
